Welcome to Beyond Stellar

Space exploration has been a milestone in human history, representing our innate curiosity for the unknown and our desire to understand our place in the universe.

Beyond Stellar is a platform dedicated to celebrating and explore the vast and fascinating cosmos around us.

From the first space flights to today's exciting projects and futuristic visions, our website offers a comprehensive view of space exploration.

Discover historical achievements, explore the latest scientific discoveries, learn about. to the pioneers of space exploration and contemplate the wonders of the universe that are yet to be discovered.

Join us on this exciting journey beyond the Earth's borders and immerse yourself in the infinite cosmos waiting to be explored.

Current missions

  • Mars, goal for 2040
    Passing through the Earth's satellite is an intermediate point towards the great objective: reaching the red planet. There, in the rugged lands of Mars, the Perseverance rover has been located since February 2021, analyzing all possible samples, until the arrival of humans.
  • Moon, goal September 2025
    The first crewed Artemis mission around the Moon, and September 2026 for Artemis III, which is expected to land the first astronauts near the lunar South Pole.
